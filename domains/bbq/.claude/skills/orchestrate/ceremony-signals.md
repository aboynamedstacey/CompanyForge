# Ceremony Signals — Competitive BBQ

## Tiers

**Trivial:** Quick question, known answer. "What temp do I pull brisket?" Builder alone, no review.

**Small:** Single recipe or single-protein cook plan. "Build me a rub for spare ribs." Builder + Verifier.

**Medium:** Full protocol development with review. "Develop a complete rib cook protocol for KCBS." Scout + Builder + Verifier + Reviewer (three-pass). One human gate.

**Large:** Multi-category competition prep. "Full comp prep — 4 categories for Saturday's KCBS." Full pipeline, parallel category planning, cross-category scheduling, multiple reviews, two human gates.

When in doubt, look at these signals: how many proteins, how many categories, competition stakes, timeline complexity, equipment involved. Score to the highest tier matching 2+ signals.

## Tier Staffing

| Tier | Capabilities | Human Gates |
|---|---|---|
| Trivial | Builder | None |
| Small | Builder + Verifier | None |
| Medium | Scout + Builder + Verifier + Reviewer | 1 (before delivery) |
| Large | Scout + Builder + Verifier + Reviewer (parallel per category) | 2 (after strategy + before delivery) |

## Budget Defaults
- Trivial: $0.25 | Small: $1 | Medium: $10 | Large: $50

## Deep Research Trigger
Set deep_research = true when: unfamiliar competition body, high-altitude cooking (3500ft+), extreme weather conditions, unfamiliar protein or equipment, national-level competition, or user explicitly asks.
