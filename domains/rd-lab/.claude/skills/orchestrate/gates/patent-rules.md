# Patent Search Rules

Patent search is for LANDSCAPE AWARENESS only.

DO:
- Surface what exists: "These patents cover similar approaches: [list]"
- Flag directly relevant patents for human + patent counsel review
- Note patent filing dates and assignees

DO NOT:
- Interpret claim scope
- Assess infringement likelihood
- Advise on design-arounds
- Recommend patent strategy (filing, licensing, etc.)

If a patent appears directly relevant, BANK for human review (in both
attended and unattended modes).
